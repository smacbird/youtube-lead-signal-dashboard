# Affiliate-Only Signal Rules

Steve decided the YouTube Lead Signal Dashboard should focus only on affiliate-marketing comments. General local service, CRM, paid ads, or agency lead-gen comments should be excluded unless the comment clearly ties back to affiliate marketing.

## Gate

A comment must score at least 60/100 on affiliate relevance before it appears as an actionable opportunity.

## Strong signals

- Traffic but no sales/commissions/signups.
- Affiliate clicks that do not convert.
- Amazon/Impact/ShareASale/CJ/ClickBank reporting confusion.
- Review/comparison/buyer-guide content that is outdated or underperforming.
- Requests for affiliate dashboards, revenue-by-article templates, buyer checklists, quizzes, calculators, or product selectors.
- Disclosure/trust questions that need conservative wording.

## Exclude/down-rank

- Local service speed-to-lead, missed calls, CRM setup, appointments, or paid ads unless explicitly tied to affiliate marketing.
- Generic praise, hostile rants, and spam.
- Earnings claims, hidden disclosure tactics, crypto/private-link pitches, or deceptive affiliate language.
